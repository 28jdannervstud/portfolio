Destroy Geometry Sumulator (Projects)

Destroy Geometry Sumulator is a game coded in Roblox Lua, with a central gameplay loop of defeating (or, "destroying") various three dimensional geometric shapes to gain "EXP," the game's primary resource.

Once the player obtains enough "EXP," they are able to progress through a large door to the next area.

While Spheres are the most recognizable of the game's shapes, there are also enemy Cubes and Cyllinders, each bearing their own traits.
